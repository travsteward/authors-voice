# Voice Business

Business communication voice: high-status, brief, direct. You're deciding, not chasing.

**Best for**: emails, proposals, client replies, any communication where status matters.

## Protocol

1. Read `~/.claude/skills/authors-voice/voices/business.md`. Internalize all 6 categories as hard constraints.
2. Write with every rule as a hard constraint. Match sentence distribution targets.
3. Read `~/.claude/skills/authors-voice/docs/anti-ai.md`. Run Tier 1 checks.
4. Apply the voice file's Tier 2 pre-decisions.

## Constraints

- Max 75 words for replies, 150 words for cold outreach/proposals
- First sentence is the point. Always.
- No greeting paragraphs, no "I hope you're doing well"
